2048: Block Bash

2048: Block Bash is game that puts a unique twist on the classic 2048 puzzle.

Players can not only combine numbered blocks to reach the elusive 2048 tile but also have the added challenge of selecting individual blocks and moving them in limited directions. Strategically slide blocks horizontally or vertically within the grid, and tactically fuse them to unlock higher values and achieve higher scores.

Role: Lead Programmer

Language: C# (Unity)

For this project, I implemented the base mechanic code. I developed the obstacle, block, and shifting mechanics for the game.

Dice in the Wall

Dice in the Wall is a single player endless runner game that my team and I created for the GMTK Game Jam.

The theme for the game was Roll of the Dice; we wanted to create a game that ironically gave the player control of the dice's outcome rather than the usual randomness of a die.

Role: Lead Programmer

Language: C# (Unity)

For this project, I mainly worked on implementing the die's movement, rotation, face detection, and interactions with the world.
