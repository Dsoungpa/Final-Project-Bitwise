2048: Block Bash

2048: Block Bash is game that puts a unique twist on the classic 2048 puzzle.

Players can not only combine numbered blocks to reach the elusive 2048 tile but also have the added challenge of selecting individual blocks and moving them in limited directions. Strategically slide blocks horizontally or vertically within the grid, and tactically fuse them to unlock higher values and achieve higher scores.
